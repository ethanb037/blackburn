About

V Noel is a multidisciplinary creator.

His post-colonial sound identity explores universal expressions of urban phenomenon.

His primary expression lies in presenting the anthropological authenticity in socio-political contradictions.

He focuses on the tension between tradition and familiarity.

Coming from a diverse background, he has studied physics, computation, and music therapy & education.

He is currently studying composition with Vykintas Baltakas at the Conservatorium Maastricht.
